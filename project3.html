Pigeon's Mission

About This Project

This is a pixel-style roguelike game and one of my long-term solo projects. I've been developing it for over 5 years, handling everything from art, programming, and music to game design and storytelling—all by myself. It's planned for release in 2026 and will be available across all major platforms. Stay tuned!

This is a very special project—one born from the dream of a 7-year-old boy named Logan from the United States. Logan loves playing video games and dreamed of turning a little pigeon he designed into a real game. His father, John, reached out to me to help make that dream come true.

Using Logan’s original hand-drawn sketches, I created the first prototype, which thrilled him beyond words. As development progressed, I brought in a talented artist and a level designer to form a small team with John, and together we’ve been building this game ever since.

In 2024, we launched a Kickstarter campaign and raised over $20,000 in support. In May 2025, our game proudly won the Chat’s Choice Award at the OTK Games Expo, along with a $5,000 prize.

The game is still in development and is planned for a full release across all major platforms by the end of 2025. Stay tuned—Logan’s dream is becoming a reality.
